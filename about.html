How it works

First Contact

After your initial contact with us, one of our representatives will get right back to you as soon as possible. From there an initial consultation will be scheduled in where we can discuss your idea, problems, and possible solutions. Our consultations can be scheduled through email and conducted through video conferencing or a call.

The Meeting

During the consultation you'll get to meet our team and receive an overview of our internal processes. We'll discuss what you're trying to accomplish, what problems you may be facing, and your overall goals. We'll go over pricing, timelines, and exactly what you can expect from the process. We're here to answer your questions, concerns, and comments. We take pride in helping you create your ideas, and in order to achieve your vision, we'll go over the project as a whole, primary purpose, goals, and your ideas.

The Sprint!

After the consultation, we'll begin working on your project! Our workflow is split up into multiple week long sprints, after each sprint we'll re-evaluate, present our prototypes, and allow you to provide feedback in time for the next sprint. We update you throughout every step of the process so you're never wondering how far along we are. Your voice matters and we're always available to answer questions or concerns.

Finish Line!

We've completed your product! From here we send you the finished product. Our team is always here in case you need us, we'll place you on our clients list and you'll receive priority support. We hope to be able to complete more of your projects soon!
